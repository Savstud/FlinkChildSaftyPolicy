Child Safety Standards for Flink

Introduction

At Flink, we are committed to creating a safe and secure environment for all our users. This page outlines our standards and practices aimed at preventing child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) within our app. We prioritize child safety and adhere to all relevant legal and safety standards to ensure that our platform is free from harmful content.

Our Commitment to CSAE Prevention

Flink takes a proactive approach to preventing CSAE and harmful behavior. We have implemented the following safety measures:

  • Monitoring and reporting: We provide in-app tools to report harmful content or users.
  • Moderation policies: We use a combination of automated systems and manual moderation to detect and remove inappropriate content.
  • Age restrictions: Flink is intended for users over the age of 18. We require verified email addresses for account creation, ensuring only authorized users can access the platform.
  • Education and training: Our team undergoes regular training to handle CSAE reports and to ensure the safety of our community.

How to Report CSAE Concerns

If you encounter any content or behavior that you believe violates child safety standards, please report it immediately. We take every report seriously and aim to address any issues as quickly as possible.

You can report any concerns by:

  • Using the in-app report function.
  • Contacting our support team at flinksocialnetwork@gmail.com.

Contact Information

If you have any questions regarding our child safety policies, please reach out to our designated contact:
